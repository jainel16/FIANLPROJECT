Fundamentals of Computing introduces the core concepts of computers and how they work. It covers topics like hardware, software, algorithms, and programming basics. Students learn problem-solving skills and how to apply logical thinking to real-world computing tasks. This foundation prepares learners for advanced studies in computer science and technology.
